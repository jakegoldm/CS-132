Hi There!

My name is Jake Goldman and I'm a current sophomore at Caltech. I grew up in Warrenton, Virginia and attended Fauquier High School from 2017 through 2021. Throughout my time in high school, I was a member of the swim team and National Honors Society. Since, getting to Caltech, I have continued swimming while pursuing my undergraduate degree in Computer Science. Although sometimes I find that my studies can be a bit exhausting - even when I'm at the pool!

In terms of academics, I have a particular interests in networks and cybersecurity. I aim to pursue a PhD in cybersecurity following my undergraduate degree and research new methods for designing secure software, particularly those that deal with networking.
