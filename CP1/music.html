What do I listen to?

My favorite genre of music to listen to is punk rock. I enjoy songs that are really powerful and intense. I have a wide array of subgenres and other music types that can be seen below.

I really like to discover new music and subgenres. I spend a lot of my time just exploring different playlists and albums I find on Spotify. Nevertheless, there are still several artists that I listen to the most. Within the last four months, my top artists were:

  1. The Offspring
  2. Dropkick Murphys
  3. blink-182
  4. One Step Closer
  5. Green Day
  6. Keshi
  7. My Chemical Romance
  8. Flogging Molly
  9. Bad Religion
  10. Pennywise

Concerts I've been to recently

  • Keshi

    April, 2023

    Keshi is a lofi artist who has a lot of very instrumental and acoustic songs. I saw him perform at the Greek Theater with some friends. The concert was a great time and he put on a really great show!

  • The Offspring

    February, 2023

    This was arguably the most I've looked forward to any concert. The Offspring is a rock band that formed in the late 80's and still tours to this day. I went to see them perform at the Belasco Theater in Downtown LA and the show was amazing. The only downside was having to wait in line in the pouring rain for what felt like forever, since we wanted to get there as early as possible to be close to the stage. The show was amazing and one of the most fun concerts I've ever been to.

  • Thy Art is Murder & Kublai Khan

    February, 2023

    I didn't know either of these bands super well going into the show. Both of them are death metal, which I don't typically listen to, but still enjoy from time to time. Kublai Khan put on a really good opener, but we showed up about halfway through their set and didn't get to experience all they had to offer. By the time Thy Art is Murder came on, I managed to make it to the rail, which made the show even more exciting.

  • Joyce Manor, PUP, Jeff Rosenstock, & Slaughterhouse

    January, 2023

    This was the best concert I've ever been to. All four performances were great and brought a lot of energy to the crowd. The entire show was a little over four hours, so I was pretty exhausted by the end. It paid off well, however, as Joyce Manor's set was a lot of fun to experience live.

  • Rina Sawayama

    November, 2023

    Rina is a great artist and has a lot of versitality. The concert was a really great time. I hadn't listened to a ton of her music before going to the concert, but I still found it really enjoyable and would definitely go to another one of her shows!

  • Cannibal Corpse

    November, 2023

    This was the first metal concert I had ever been to, so I wasn't sure what to expect going in. It was a good concert and a great introduction into what metal had to offer. I was pretty far back in the crowd for most of the show, since it was my first time in that type of environment.
