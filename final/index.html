About Us

JakeOS aims to combine the powers of Artificial Intelligence (AI) and cybersecurity to provide constantly evolving and improving computing system. Our technology brings a new computing system that provides layered encryption in networking, relayed connections, isolated memory, as well as a built-in learning system that identifies vulnerabilities based on your activity and consistently improves itself.
