One Million!

You have faced a long and arduous journey...

After the fall of your kingdom to the Great Darkness, you have trekked across the land to defeat the Evil Dog – overseer of the Great Darkness. Along the way, you have assembled a party that you hope is capable of defeating the Dog and restoring peace to your land. Your party must face the Dog and defeat him in order to win. The Dog, however, has a health of 1,000,000 points.

The Hero. Capable of dealing moderate amounts of damage with a moderate amount of defense.

The Knight. Deals massive amounts of damage but has weak defense.

The Scholar. Deals a weak amount of damage but has a high defense. When dealing any damage, they'll heal a portion of your team's HP.

The Mage. Incapable of dealing any damage but has moderate defense. The Mage may upgrade the stats for any party member.

One each turn, all members of your team will have the opportunity to act, with the dog making a move at the end of a turn. To win, you must reduce the Dog's health to 0.

Do you have what it takes?
